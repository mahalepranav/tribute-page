Tribute To Dr. Abdul Kalam

Born 15 Oct 1931 Died 27 Jul 2015

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and
statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. Born and
raised in a Muslim family in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he studied physics and
aerospace engineering.
